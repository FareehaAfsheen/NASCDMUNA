About NASCDMUN

Navy Anchorage School & College Dhaka Model United Nations is a premier diplomatic simulation bringing together Bangladesh's brightest young minds. We provide a platform to debate real-world issues, develop leadership, and build global awareness.

Our conference is designed to challenge delegates intellectually while fostering collaboration, diplomacy, and critical thinking at the highest level.
